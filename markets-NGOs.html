NGOs & IGOs

Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) and Intergovernmental Organizations (IGOs)

We support Intergovernmental Organizations and Non-Governmental Organizations in their mission to maintain international peace and security, drive and expand sustainable development and take action against terrorism and instability, improve people's living standards, provide sustainable energy and water and support human rights by countering human trafficking amongst many other measures.

Ecolog provides support solutions to NGOs and IGOs in case of emergency response, deployment to disaster areas and war zones, refugee camps construction and management, and in regions where long-term intervention is needed due to prolonged deprivation.

We construct, equip, and manage temporary accommodations and provide all necessary facilities and services including food supply and preparation, potable water, medical infrastructure, waste management and supply chain and logistics.
