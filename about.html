About Us

Everyday, Everywhere, Glocos Trading

  • Enabling our clients to focus on their operation by taking care of their needs, is our leading philosophy at Glocos Trading BV . Providing services and solutions in a wide range of environments, including some of the world’s most remote and challenging locations, is our commitment.

  • Our determination to create and maintain comfort, even in a daily changing environment and to provide our customers with customized turnkey solutions, drove us to further expand our portfolio from a single service provider to a full-fledged integrated and smart service provider for large operations; revolutionizing a dispersed market into a united one.

  • Our experience, dedication and expertise has enabled us to become the most reliable partner in providing food supply chain, catering services. Following the development of the markets, we conceptualized the challenges that arose with it and developed solutions.
